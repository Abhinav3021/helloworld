Expectations :-

In JEE prepration days ,I wish to take admission in top IITs in India and influenced by their campus and facilities they give to the students.

Campus:-

Beautiful and huge campus , canteen available 24x7, party clubs disco, decent girls-boys ratio.

No Attendance issue:-

Life is chill after clearing JEE and there is no such attendance criteria. We have proper time to explore new feilds to make our interests and build passion. Just to go college for exams and quizes.

Excellent Sports:-

Every single sports is present in our campus and we can easily explore the interested sports and professors encourage students to participate events.

Reality:-

I face Reality when I came here and join the campus and found that temporary campus is decent. Hostals are good and mess is decent.

Campus:-

Facing problems of temporary campus, only 40% of JUT campus is accessable by us. No canteen (only mess), No party clubs, Continuous electricity power cut, limited or no fest, girls:boys ratio 5%, library open till 6 pm and books are not issued.

75% attendance compulsary

We have to mantain the attendance upto 75% ,so that we can sit in exams. Lots of continuous classes which cause headache.

Sports:-

Every sport is casual.Badminton,vollyball, cricket,table tennis are the games which are temporarily present in our temporary campus. Both outdoor and indoor games are hindered due to electricity issue in evening and night.
